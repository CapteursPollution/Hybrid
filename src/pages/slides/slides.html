Welcome to ClearSky !

With the next few pages we’ll try and help you setup your multi-pollutions sensing system.

Depending on your familiarity with the Raspberry Pi, the process may take up to 15 minutes.

Setting up the Raspberry Pi

First up, you need to get a Raspberry Pi with internet connectivity.

  • The latest model (Raspberry Pi 3B) comes equipped with Wifi and ethernet
  • The Pi 0 also comes with Wifi, but is slower
  • Finally, you can also buy a Wifi adapter and plug it into any Raspberry Pi

We recommend heading over to https://projects.raspberrypi.org for a quick tutorial on getting started.
